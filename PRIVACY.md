# Privacy Policy — LinkedIn Focus

LinkedIn Focus does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any user data.

- The extension runs only on `linkedin.com` and makes no network requests of its own.
- It stores two small values in the browser's local storage (`localStorage`), scoped to linkedin.com:
  - a save-streak counter (how many times the feed was blocked, per day, for the last 7 days)
  - a temporary "unlock until" timestamp for the optional 5-minute feed unlock
- This data never leaves your device. It is not sent to the developer, to LinkedIn, or to any third party. It is not used for advertising, analytics, or tracking of any kind.
- Uninstalling the extension removes this data along with everything else the extension stored.
